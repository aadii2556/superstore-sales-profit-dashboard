# SuperStore Sales & Profit Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview
An enterprise-grade retail analytics dashboard built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer distribution, and regional business trends across the United States.

This project transforms raw retail transactional data into actionable business intelligence using KPI engineering, interactive visualizations, geo-spatial analytics, and drill-down reporting.

## Key Features

- Executive KPI monitoring
- Interactive state-level filtering
- Category-wise sales and profit analysis
- Geo-spatial customer visualization
- Drill-down matrix reporting
- Profitability intelligence
- Dynamic business insights

---

## Business Problem

Retail organizations often struggle to:

- Identify underperforming regions
- Monitor profitability across categories
- Analyze customer concentration geographically
- Generate executive-level analytical reports

This dashboard centralizes retail analytics into a single interactive business intelligence platform.

- Geographic customer concentration is strongest in major commercial regions.
- Certain categories exhibit unstable profit margins across states.
